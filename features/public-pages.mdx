## About page `/om-os`

Introduces the gym and its story.

- **Hero** — page header with the title "Om Os" and a background image.
- **Intro section** — welcome text: *"Xtreme Fitness er stedet, hvor sved, grin og god musik går hånd i hånd."*
- **Stats + video section** — four stats (600K+ work hours, 790+ successful programmes, 2560+ happy customers, 830+ healthier bodies) alongside a video embed.
- **Contact section** — inline contact form.
- **Latest blogs** — the three most recent blog posts.

## Blog list `/blog`

An index of all published blog posts.

- **Hero** — page header with the title "Blog".
- **Featured post** — the first post in the list is displayed as a large hero card with its cover image, title, and teaser. Clicking it navigates to `/blog/:blogId`.
- **All posts grid** — every post rendered in a repeating grid via `BlogPostsSection` with the heading "Alle Blogindlæg".

<Tip>
  When no posts exist, a notice is displayed instead of the featured card. Fetch errors also surface inline in the same location.
</Tip>

---

## Blog detail `/blog/:blogId`

Renders a single blog post identified by its `blogId` URL parameter.

- **Hero** — uses the post's own cover image when available, falling back to the services header image. The hero title is the post title.
- **Post body** — displays the post's cover image, title, teaser, and rich-text content. The `content` field is sanitised with **DOMPurify** before being injected via `dangerouslySetInnerHTML`, so admin-authored HTML is safe to render.
- **Error state** — if the post cannot be found or fetched, a notice is shown with a **"Tilbage til blog"** link back to `/blog`.

---

## Contact page `/kontakt`

A dedicated contact page. It renders a standalone contact form where visitors can send a message directly to the gym. Submitted messages appear in the admin inbox at `/admin/messages`.
